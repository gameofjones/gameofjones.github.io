One Card is All You Need

Whether you commute in the region by bus, ferry, rail or train - or a combination. ORCA is accepted on Community Transit, Everett Transit, King County Metro Transit, Kitsap Transit, Pierce Transit, Sound Transit and Washington State Ferries.

Tap and pause. You will see a green light and hear the beep.

Bus: Tap at the ORCA card reader when you board.
Rapid Ride: Tap at the ORCA card reader before you board.
Ferry: Tap at the turnstile. Or, staff can tap your card on their card reader.
Street Car: Tap on a station platform reader before you board.
Train: Tap at the ORCA card reader on the station platform prior to boarding.

Tap off when you exit so the correct fare is calculated.
